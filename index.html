I created this site in January 2021 to track a suspiciously familiar new feature on the Kermode and Mayo's Film Review show on BBC Radio 5 Live (known to its fans as Wittertainment).

Listener Sarah Lynch emailed the show on 15 January (listen here at 22:37), saying she and her husband were planning a virtual road trip around the United States in February. On 22 January we set off from Washington state on our lockdown tour, with listeners contributing a film for each state as we go.

A couple of weeks later, on 5 February , this site was announced to the world! (Listen on SoundCloud or here at 01:14:19.)
